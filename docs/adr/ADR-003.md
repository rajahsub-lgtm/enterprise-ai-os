# ADR-003: Enterprise Reasoning Framework

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Enterprise AI systems must make recommendations that are transparent,
repeatable, explainable, and auditable.

Organizations have invested decades refining proven reasoning
methodologies such as:

- Kepner-Tregoe
- Five Whys
- Fishbone (Ishikawa)
- FMEA
- OODA
- Lean
- Six Sigma

Rather than replacing these methodologies with opaque AI inference,
Enterprise AI Operating System (EAIOS) should orchestrate and augment
them.

---

## Decision

EAIOS shall introduce an Enterprise Reasoning Framework.

The framework shall:

- Treat reasoning as a first-class enterprise capability.
- Support multiple reasoning methods.
- Allow reasoning methods to be added without changing the enterprise operating model.
- Allow AI to augment reasoning rather than replace enterprise decision methodologies.

Reasoning methods are enterprise assets.

Artificial Intelligence is one implementation technology capable of
assisting those methods.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- Explainable recommendations
- Auditable decisions
- Vendor independence
- Extensible reasoning architecture
- Human governance remains central

### Tradeoffs

- Requires reasoning abstractions.
- Requires selection of appropriate reasoning methods.
- Introduces additional enterprise modeling.

---

## Architectural Principle

Enterprise Intelligence is built on enterprise reasoning—not AI reasoning.
