# ADR-003: Enterprise Reasoning Framework

## Status

Accepted

**Date:** 2026-06-24

---

# Context

Enterprise AI systems must produce recommendations that are transparent, repeatable, explainable, and auditable.

Organizations have invested decades developing proven reasoning methodologies, including:

* Kepner-Tregoe
* Five Whys
* Fishbone (Ishikawa)
* FMEA
* OODA
* Lean
* Six Sigma

These methods represent valuable organizational knowledge and should not be replaced by opaque AI inference.

Instead, Enterprise AI should augment and orchestrate enterprise reasoning.

---

# Decision

EAIOS adopts an Enterprise Reasoning Framework as the primary operating model for enterprise decision-making.

Enterprise Reasoning is treated as a first-class enterprise capability.

The Enterprise Reasoning Framework transforms enterprise intelligence into explainable, governed recommendations.

Enterprise Reasoning consists of the following stages:

```text id="ahoc5o"
Observation
        ↓
Evidence
        ↓
Evidence Fusion
        ↓
Hypothesis Generation
        ↓
Hypothesis Ranking
        ↓
Operational Confidence
        ↓
Execution Strategy
        ↓
Recommendation
```

Reasoning methods are applied within this framework.

---

# Evidence Fusion

Evidence Fusion is the first activity of Enterprise Reasoning.

Evidence may originate from:

* Current observations
* Enterprise Memory
* Enterprise Knowledge
* Historical execution
* Business context

Enterprise reasoning reconciles multiple sources of evidence before conclusions are drawn.

---

# Reasoning Methods

The Enterprise Reasoning Framework supports multiple reasoning methodologies.

Examples include:

* Kepner-Tregoe
* Five Whys
* Fishbone (Ishikawa)
* FMEA
* OODA
* Lean
* Six Sigma
* Future enterprise reasoning methods

Reasoning methods are enterprise assets.

New methods may be introduced without changing the enterprise operating model.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence augments enterprise reasoning.

AI may assist by:

* Generating hypotheses
* Retrieving knowledge
* Summarizing evidence
* Identifying patterns
* Supporting decision analysis

Enterprise reasoning remains responsible for transforming intelligence into explainable, governed recommendations.

AI enhances enterprise reasoning.

It does not replace it.

---

# Consequences

## Benefits

* Explainable recommendations
* Transparent decision-making
* Auditable reasoning
* Vendor independence
* Extensible reasoning architecture
* Human governance remains central
* Consistent enterprise decision processes

---

## Trade-offs

* Requires reasoning abstractions
* Requires selection of appropriate reasoning methods
* Introduces additional enterprise modeling
* Requires disciplined evidence management

---

# Architectural Principles

Enterprise intelligence becomes valuable through enterprise reasoning.

Reasoning transforms collective intelligence into explainable, governed action.

Operational Confidence determines the depth of reasoning required.

Evidence is reconciled before conclusions are drawn.

Reasoning methods evolve.

The enterprise reasoning operating model remains stable.
