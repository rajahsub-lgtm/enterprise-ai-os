# ADR-002: Business Outcome First Architecture

## Status

Accepted

**Date:** 2026-06-23

---

## Context

Many AI initiatives begin with technology selection, model selection, agent development, or chatbot implementation.

This often results in isolated solutions that are difficult to govern, reuse, measure, and scale across the enterprise.

Organizations do not invest in AI to build agents.

Organizations invest in AI to achieve business outcomes.

An enterprise operating model should therefore begin with the desired business outcome and work backwards to identify the capabilities, skills, knowledge, agents, humans, tools, and workflows required to achieve it.

---

## Decision

EAIOS shall adopt a Business Outcome First architecture.

All enterprise AI capabilities shall be traceable to a measurable business outcome.

The primary architectural decomposition shall be:

```text
Business Outcome
→ Capability
→ Skill
→ Implementation
```

Implementations may consist of:

* AI Agents
* Humans
* Tools
* Workflows
* Automation
* External Services

Capabilities represent collections of reusable skills.

Skills represent reusable enterprise assets.

Implementations may evolve over time without changing the business outcome, capability, or skill model.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

* Strong alignment to business value
* Improved governance and prioritization
* Greater reuse across solutions
* Reduced technology-driven architecture decisions
* Clear traceability from implementation to outcome

### Tradeoffs

* Requires capability and skill modeling
* Introduces additional architectural abstraction
* Requires business outcome definitions and ownership

---

## Example

Application Health Monitoring:

```text
Business Outcome
Maintain Application Health

    ↓

Capability
Application Health Management

    ↓

Skills
- Telemetry Collection
- Knowledge Retrieval
- Pattern Recognition
- Problem Analysis
- Recommendation Generation

    ↓

Implementations
- Telemetry Agent
- Knowledge Agent
- Reasoning Agent
- Recommendation Agent
- Human Operator
```

---

## Architectural Principle

Business outcomes drive capabilities.

Capabilities are built from reusable skills.

Implementations are replaceable.

Enterprise value is measured by outcomes, not agents.
