# ADR-001: Build an Enterprise AI Operating System

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-06-18

---

## Context

Enterprise AI is currently being implemented as isolated applications and chatbots. While these solutions demonstrate technical capability, they often lack a common operating model for governance, reuse, observability, lifecycle management, and enterprise integration.

At the same time, classical multi-agent systems research provides concepts such as beliefs, intentions, collaboration, and goal-directed behavior that remain highly relevant but have not been fully operationalized in enterprise environments.

---

## Decision

Build a cloud-agnostic Enterprise AI Operating System (EAIOS).

The platform will provide reusable enterprise capabilities including:

- Persistent Goal Management
- Multi-Agent Orchestration
- Enterprise Knowledge Fabric
- Agent Registry
- Agent Portfolio Management
- Governance
- Observability
- AI Resilience

Business capabilities will be composed from reusable AI capabilities rather than building isolated AI applications.

Cloud providers and LLM vendors will be accessed through adapters.

---

## Consequences

### Benefits

- Vendor independence
- Reusable architecture
- Enterprise governance
- Explainability
- Extensible platform
- Research foundation for future publication

### Trade-offs

- Greater initial design effort
- More abstraction than point solutions
- Requires disciplined architecture

---

## Guiding Principle

## Strategic Principles

### Reuse

> Build AI once. Reuse it everywhere.

### Collective Intelligence

EAIOS treats collective intelligence as a first-class enterprise capability.

The platform continuously measures, governs, and improves an organization's ability to solve business outcomes through reusable capabilities, skills, agents, humans, tools, workflows, and enterprise knowledge.

Business outcomes drive capabilities.

Capabilities are composed of reusable skills.

Skills may be implemented by multiple agents, humans, tools, or workflows and are continuously evaluated and improved through learning and experimentation.
