\# EAIOS Version 1 Scope



\## Mission



Demonstrate that EAIOS can safely orchestrate multiple AI agents to achieve a business outcome through capability-based execution, explainable reasoning, governed decision-making, and continuous enterprise learning.



\---



\# In Scope



\## Business Outcomes



Business outcomes are the entry point for all EAIOS execution.



Examples:



\* Maintain Application Health

\* Restore Service Availability

\* Reduce Incident Volume



\---



\## Capability Assessment



Determine whether an AI capability exists that can support a business outcome.



Possible outcomes:



\* Capability Exists

\* Capability Does Not Exist



Capability gaps are recorded for future consideration.



\---



\## Capability-Based Execution



Capabilities define the enterprise AI functionality available to achieve business outcomes.



Example:



Application Health Management



\---



\## Task Planning



Capabilities are decomposed into executable tasks.



Example:



\* Collect Telemetry

\* Analyze Logs

\* Search Knowledge

\* Perform Reasoning

\* Recommend Action



\---



\## Skill Matching



Tasks are mapped to required skills.



Example:



\* Log Analysis

\* Knowledge Retrieval

\* KT Problem Analysis

\* Risk Assessment



\---



\## Agent Orchestration



Agents implementing required skills are selected and orchestrated.



\---



\## Safety and Governance



All execution must be:



\* Explainable

\* Governed

\* Observable

\* Auditable



Human approval may be required.



\---



\## Execution Trace



Execution trace records:



\* Business Outcome

\* Capability

\* Tasks

\* Skills

\* Agents

\* Reasoning Methods

\* Safety Decisions

\* Recommendations

\* Outcomes



\---



\## Enterprise Learning



Capture:



\* Evidence

\* Reasoning outcomes

\* Human interventions

\* Safety outcomes

\* Improvement opportunities



\---



\# Out of Scope



The following are explicitly deferred.



\## Agent Catalog



Agent inventory and lifecycle management.



\## AI Asset Management



Portfolio management of AI assets.



\## AgentOps



Advanced observability, telemetry, cost, performance, and operational analytics.



\## Cost Optimization



Optimization of execution cost and resource utilization.



\## Portfolio Analytics



Capability ROI and investment planning.



\## Enterprise-Wide Orchestration



Orchestration of non-AI enterprise resources.
