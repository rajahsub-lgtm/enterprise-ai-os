# EAIOS Architectural Laws

Architectural laws define principles that are expected to remain stable as the framework evolves.

---

## Law 1

### Business Outcomes Over Agent Implementations

Every architectural decision must improve an enterprise capability that delivers measurable business outcomes.

AI agents are implementations.

Enterprise capabilities are the enduring architectural contract.

---

## Law 2

### Stable Capabilities, Replaceable Implementations

Enterprise capabilities evolve slowly.

Implementations evolve continuously.

Technology should be replaceable without changing the enterprise capability model.

---

## Law 3

### Enterprise Language Before AI Language

Use industry-standard enterprise terminology whenever possible.

Invent new terminology only when describing genuinely new architectural concepts.

Innovation belongs in the architecture, not the vocabulary.
